Why

At STV, fresh grads get real exposure to venture and startups from day one—not shadowing, but doing. You’ll see how deals get sourced, evaluated, and closed, and how we support founders. It’s the fastest way to learn if VC or building is your path.

Who

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

Fresh graduates or students graduating in 2026 who are eager to learn business, VC, and tech. We welcome all backgrounds—tech, finance, engineering, or other—as long as you’re curious and want to learn.

Where is the internship based?

Our internship is based in Riyadh. You should be based or willing to relocate to Riyadh for the duration of the internship.

What does the internship involve?

You’ll support the investment team with research, due diligence, and portfolio work. You’ll get exposure to how we source deals, evaluate startups, and support founders—with real responsibility and feedback.

How long is the internship?

Duration and start dates are discussed during the process. We’ll share more details when we get in touch.

When is the deadline?

Applications open February 17th. The deadline is March 3rd (2 weeks from opening). Apply before the deadline to be considered for the next cohort.
